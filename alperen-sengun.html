Alperen Sengun- Professional Career

Later, he began playing youth basketball with Giresun University’s youthful teams. He moved to Bandirma after graduating from university in 2014 and joined Bandirma Kirmizi B.K. He stayed for more than five years and accomplished a great lot. Among those achievements were winning the Turkish Basketball Junior League championship and debuting with the Turkish junior national team.

For the 2019-20 season, he was signed by Teksut Bandirma, a Turkish Super League top-tier squad. Following that, he was given the opportunity to play for Turkey’s junior under-18 national team in the 2019 FIBA Under-18 European Championship.

At the conclusion of the FIBA competition, the Turkish team was awarded a silver medal. Sengun was also the only Turkish player to be named to the All-Tournament Team. He returned to Teksut Bandirma after the 2019 FIBA Under-18 European Championship to resume his duties. He performed admirably there, earning him a spot in the 2019–20 FIBA Champions League. His numbers, however, were insufficient for the squad to keep him for another season. As a result, he was released at the end of the 2019-20 season.

Later, in August 2020, he agreed to terms with Besiktas Basketbol on a three-year contract. Since then, he has been performing for the team and achieving amazing results. In addition, he is now playing for the Houston Rockets after signing a contract on August 7, 2021.
