Who Is Father Philis? His Bio, Age, Country, Real Name, TikTok Sweet Girl

Often times we see many people jumping on the bandwagon of a new trend on TikTok. Some months ago it was the “Bing Bong” trend while a year ago people were crazy over the “Buss It Challenge.” Recently, anyone who has been browsing through TikTok has at least once come across the trending song called “Sweet Girl”. In this article, we will be talking about Father Philis, the man behind the viral TikTok song “Sweet Girl”.

Discover his age, net worth, dating life, and more below.

Meet Father Philis, The Hitmaker Behind The Viral TikTok Song “Sweet Girl”

While there are various trends that pop up on TikTok every day, Sweet Girl is definitely leading the charts by quite a margin. The official name of the sound is “Sweet Giirrrrllll face beat x, Father Philis” and as of May 25th, there were already over 350 thousand videos made using this sound.

Sweet Girl has everything that it takes to get trendy in TikTok–be it super edgy lyrics or a catchy beat. Father Philis, the creator of this sensational sound, blew up on the platform last year too with his song “Brawling.”

The most popular video made using the “Sweet Giirrrrllll face beat x, Father Philis” sound comes from a user named @BellanJanie. In the video, the TikTok user does some dance moves to this popular sound while biting her lips. The video has already garnered over 5.2 million likes in such a short time. Another popular video made using this sound comes from a user named @TatikenneBrew and in the video, she showed off the steps her daughter took to make her own prom dress, which popular found super adorable and hence so many views and likes.

Father Philis Age

Sweet Girl singer Father Philis’s age was 25 as of May 2022 and he celebrates his birthday on the 11th of August if Twitter is anything to go by.

Father Philis Country

Father Philis is originally from Barbados. However, he currently splits his time between Barbados and New York City.

What Is Father Philis Real Name?

Father Philis’ real name is Philip King. About his family, his mother celebrates her birthday on November 25th and his dad on January 8th. Barbados native has mentioned his parents for quite some time on Twitter.

Father Philis Net Worth

As of May 2022, Father Philis’ net worth was estimated under $200 thousand.

The Barbadian artist first rose to popularity in the summer of 2021 after his song “Brawling” went viral on all social platforms, including YouTube and TikTok. For what it’s worth, the video has racked up more than two million views on YouTube.

He gained international fame after popular R&B singer Rihanna reposted his song to her socials.

After a massive and unexpected hit of “Brawling,” he even toured in New York City for over a month, according to jamaicaobserver.com. Sweet Girl singer revealed he performed with DJ Warlock at his Brawling Day fete and with Bunji Garlin at the Soca Explosion.

“I can’t really complain but I’m trying to work with the producer remotely to keep on doing the dubplates. I will be here for another month or so,” he told BUZZ circa June 2021.

Around that time, jamaicaobserver.com reveals that the Barbadian artist was also booked for back-to-back shows with some of the most popular musicians including Caribbean singer Patrice Roberts.

His newfound fame did not only give him the gigs but he also collaborated with the various artists after “Brawling” went huge. His most famous collab was with Jamaica-raised artist Klassik Frescobar on the track called Back Seat on the Curry Stew Riddim.

Over a short period of time, he has released quite a many songs. Some of his popular singles are Face Beat, Brawling, Bubbies & Backshots, Gyal Tek Muh Money, GPS, and of course Brawling and Sweet Girl.

You can request his Performances, Promotion, Drops or Plates by filling a Bookings Request Form up on cognitoforms.com. Sweet Girl songwriter also has his own merch lineup, which consists of some awesome tees and hoodies. The prices of his products go from $25 all the way to $45.

Is Father Philis Married Or Still Dating?

Father Philis is very active on Twitter and posts dating-related stuff quite regularly on the platform. On December 10, 2012, Philip King revealed that he wants his girlfriend to be traditional, who could bake for him when he is sick.

On November 24, 2014, he made another tweet and revealed that he was single and did not have a girlfriend. Father Philis joked that his marriage got arranged on February 27, 2020.

On March 29, 2021, Father Philis tweeted “Speed dating tonight.” Later, in November that year, Philip King took to Twitter and shared his views on dating and relationships. He wrote, “Women keep dating villains then label us all the same.”

As of this writing, we are not sure if Father Philis is single or dating anyone. What we are sure of, however, is that he was not married to anyone as of May 2022.
