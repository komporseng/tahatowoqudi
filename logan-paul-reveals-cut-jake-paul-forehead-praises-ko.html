Logan Paul reveals deep cut to Jake Paul's forehead as he praises brother's sixth round KO of Tyron

Jake Paul’s brother Logan has revealed a nasty looking cut on the YouTuber-turned-boxer’s head following his brutal knockout win over Tyron Woodley.

WARNING: This article contains graphic imagery

Jake is now 5-0 (4 KOs) in his boxing career, having sent former UFC champion Woodley spiralling down to the canvas with a deadly sixth round right hook.

The rematch in Florida ended with a blockbuster finish befitting of the build up, but Jake didn’t come away scot free.

The 24-year-old American received an accidental Woodley elbow to the head in round three, and despite getting cut, he battled on to win the bout.

However, Jake was lucky to stay out of Woodley’s reach following the cut, with his brother showing the extent of the nasty looking gash post-fight.

Approaching his brother’s corner, the fellow YouTuber videoed himself saying, “I’m a doctor,” before filming his sibling's open wound and sarcastically saying, “You’re fine, it’s fine.”

A later video from Logan showed a hyped Jake patched up back in his dressing room, why a pair of hefty looking plasters covering over the cut.

Logan also Tweeted a shot of three of his Jake's knockouts in the ring, clearly impressed with his brother's achievements.

The fight, which occurred in place of Jake’s scheduled bout with the injured Tommy Fury, further boosted the YouTuber’s prospects in the sport.

He even began eyeing up potential future opponents, challenging Nate Diaz and Jorge Masvidal who were both ringside.

Jake said: “Y’all are some b****** for leaving this arena. I know you don’t want that s***.

“I’ll take out both of y’all next. Get out of your contracts with daddy Dana. I’mma f*** them up too.”
