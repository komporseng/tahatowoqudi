Josh Gad Share Tha Sarrow Of Losing His Nephew Marco

Josh Gad just lost his nephew Marco, who died in his sleep when he was 20 years old. Gad conveyed the news of his nephew’s death on June 26, expressing his sadness in a tweet,

“I appreciate all of your support. At the age of 20, our precious nephew Marco died in his sleep, his life cut much too short and our sadness far too profound. Our family is still trying to make sense of it all, but your help has been invaluable.”

Celebrities such as LeVar Burton and Bradley Whitford have spoken out in favor of the Frozen actress. In another tweet, he expressed his thoughts on the aftermath of the Roe v. Wade decision, writing that he is attempting to balance his loss with all that is going on around him. As he put it,

“Sometimes you just need someone to weep on, someone to hold your hand, or someone to argue with.” I will be all of the above to any woman who is afraid today.”

He urged everyone to fight for women’s rights and promised to be there for them at all times.
