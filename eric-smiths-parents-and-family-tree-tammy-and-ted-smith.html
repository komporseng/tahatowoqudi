What Motivated Eric Smith to Kill Derric Robie?

The key question remained unanswered at the trial’s end. Smith never gave a reason for why he did what he did. However, the convicted killer explained ten years later.

He said he wanted to vent his rage on someone. Robie, regrettably, was the unfortunate victim. A year after murdering Derrick Robie, Eric Smith testified in front of a jury in front of the whole country’s press media.

Smith had speech problems and a history of outbursts as a kid, according to trial evidence. Additionally, the jury was made aware of Eric’s ongoing bullying and expulsion from school.
