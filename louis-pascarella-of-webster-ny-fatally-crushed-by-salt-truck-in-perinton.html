Louis Pascarella of Webster, NY, Fatally Crushed by Salt Truck in Perinton

Webster, NY resident Louis Pascarella, 36, was tragically killed during a routine salt delivery operation on Perinton Parkway. The incident has sent shockwaves through the community in Perinton and beyond.

Pascarella, a dedicated worker known for his diligence and commitment, met with the fatal accident while delivering salt ahead of anticipated winter weather. According to investigators, he tripped and fell under the truck, leading to this devastating event. First responders pronounced him dead at the scene.

As news of Pascarella’s tragic death spread, condolences and tributes started pouring in from friends, colleagues, and members of the community. His loss is deeply felt, leaving a void that will not be easily filled. The Perinton and Fairport communities are grieving together, offering support to each other and to Pascarella’s family during this difficult time.

Investigations into the circumstances surrounding the accident are ongoing. Our thoughts are with the family, friends, and coworkers of Louis Pascarella as they navigate through this heartbreaking loss.
