Explore Her Net Worth, &amp; Parents Details

Thai rapper Lisa rose to fame as a member of the four-woman South Korean band, Blackpink. The youngest member of Blackpink, she stars in the group alongside other members like Jennie, Rose, and Jisoo.

Lisa has been a part of the band ever since its initiation in 2016 and serves as the lead rapper, maknae, sub-vocalist, and one of the main dancers of the musical group.

Apart from singing and dancing, Lisa also plays multiple instruments- including ukelele, piano, and guitar.

Lisa Is Known For Her Street Outfits And Fashion Sense

Lisa has established herself as a fashion icon, and her fans certainly enjoy and appreciate her fashion sense.

Though she is famous for her street look- which consists of crop tops, chains, caps, and cargo pants, the artist also loves other outfits. And, with a height of over 5 feet 5 inches (1.67 m), the musician looks flawless in any outfit she wears.

Lisa dresses up in her trademark street outfit (©: TheStar)

When Lisa attends any event- be it an award show, a musical concert, or a social program- she loves to dress in beautiful dresses, including workboots, cerulean criss-cross strapped pumps, shorts, and more.

Further, Lisa has also been promoting fashionable outfits as a part of Blackpink. Different costumes and accessories of the band are available in Amazon, with the price ranging anywhere from $3 to $100.

Lisa Is The Maknae Of Blackpink

Blackpink is a Seoul-based South Korean girl band- which was founded by YG Entertainment in 2016. The group consists of four members: Kim Ji Soo (a.k.a. Jisoo), Park Chae Young (a.k.a. Rosé), Kim Jennie (a.k.a. Jennie), and Lalisa Manoban (a.k.a. Lisa).

Among Jisoo, Rosé, and Jennie, Lisa is Blackpink's 'maknae,' a Korean term that is used to refer to the youngest person of the group.

Blackpink members: (from left to right) Lisa, Jisoo, Jennie, and Rosé, at the Gaonchart Music Awards in 2017 (©: FootWearNews)

The KPop group made its debut on 8 August 2016, releasing a single album titled Square One, and has been performing worldwide ever since. Over the years, Blackpink has released several music videos, such as Kill This Love (2019), Ddu-Du Ddu-Du (2018), Whistle (2017), Stay (2017), Boombayah (2016), Stay (2016), Playing With Fire (2016), and more.

The band's most viewed music video on YouTube is Ddu-Du Ddu-Du with a billion views, while Boombayah ranks second with over 800 million hits.

Further, Blackpink has also made several albums, including Square One (2016), Square Two (2016), BLACKPINK (2017), and BLACKPINK In Your Area (2018).

Lisa's Net Worth: How Much Wealth Do 'Blackpink' Band Members Possess?

Apart from fame, Blackpink has also helped its members secure a substantial fortune for themselves. Working with the band, Lisa has accumulated a net worth amounting to $8 million, as of 2020. 

Likewise, Lisa's three co-members of the band are also worth the same amount, as per KoreaBoo. So, as a group, the four ladies have a combined net worth of $32 million.

Lisa's Early Life & Parents

A native of Bangkok, Thailand, Lisa was born as Lalisa Manoban in 1997 (age: 23) and has her birthday listed as 27 March.

She is the only child of her parents, who later separated after the birth of their daughter. After the split, Lisa's mother got married to a Swiss chef based in Thailand named Marco Brueschweiler.

Growing up, the artist was nicknamed "Pockpack;" however, the nickname didn't last as Lisa changed her first name from Pranpriya to Lalisa.
