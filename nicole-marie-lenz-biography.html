Nicole Marie Lenz Bio (Wiki)

Who is Nicole Marie Lenz?

Nicole Marie Lenz is an American former actress and model. She is now a Yoga Therapist and a Health advocate.

As a model, she was Playboy’s Playmate of the Month March in 2000.

Nicole Marie Lenz- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

She was born on 24 Jan 1980 in Cleveland, Ohio, United States. The name of her father and her mother was Theodore Lenz and Laura Lenz respectively.

Her mom was a secretary while her dad was a contractor.

Nicole’s ancestry is Swiss, German, English, and Italian and she has a sister named Emillie Lenz.

In which school did Nicole Marie Lenz attend?

Talking about her academics, she passed out of North Royalton High School in 1998.

Nicole Marie Lenz also has a Master’s degree from the University of Sedona. Moreover,  she also enrolled at the University of New York Film Academy and the University of California.

Nicole Marie Lenz- Professional Life, Career

Nicole Marie Lenz as a model was very successful. She first posed for Ford Models. Then she was featured in Playboy, Vogue, and W magazines.

Due to her amazing persona, she was numbered 94 of the “100 Sexiest Women” in 2007. Also, she was seen in advertisements for Garnier, Joe’s Jeans, Pantene, Roxy, Adidas, and more.

As an actress, she first appeared in 2001 in the TV film Rent Control. Then in 2003 and 2006, Nicole’s other projects were Confidence and TV: The Movie respectively.

Her other screen appearances are,

  • Toxic (2010)

Other Work

After leaving the entertainment industry, she founded her own company that emphasizes advanced yogic technology, DNA testing, and metaphysics. It was founded in 2014 and is known as Earth Healthcare.

Nicole Marie Lenz is also the owner of a practice management company, Elements Holistic founded in Jan 2016. She also runs an online educational program called Shakti Rise Academy.
