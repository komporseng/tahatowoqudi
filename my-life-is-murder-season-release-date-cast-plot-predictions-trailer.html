My Life is Murder Season 3 Recap:

My Life is Murder, the thrilling crime drama series that follows the clever and determined private investigator Alexa Crowe, portrayed by the incomparable Lucy Lawless, has returned for its third season on October 10, 2021. The show features an amazing cast of returning members including Ebony Vagulans, Rawiri Jobe, Joe Naufahu, Martin Henderson, and the newest addition, Tatum Warren-Ngata, as well as incredible guest stars in each episode.

Each exciting episode of the new season revolves around a different murder case. From the intriguing case of a young teacher’s death at a tango club in the first episode “It Takes Two,” to the mysterious crime novelist’s death found encased in a sculpture in episode two titled “Nothing Concrete,” and the thrilling story of a champion thoroughbred blamed for his handler’s death in a successful horse-breeding stud, Alexa Crowe is sure to keep audiences on the edge of their seats.

My Life is Murder Season 3 Review:

My Life is Murder Season 3 has thrilled and captivated audiences with its charm and colorful characters. Alexa Crowe’s effortless ability to solve murders, along with the perfect blend of humor, crime and mystery truly makes this show a gem.

My Life is Murder Season 3 Rating:

The critics have given it a 7.5/10 rating on IMDb and an impressive 87% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes. Both viewers and critics alike have been completely engrossed in the intrigue and excitement that My Life is Murder Season 3 has to offer.

My Life is Murder Season 4 Plot: What could it be about?

The series revolves around the lives of Alexa Crowe, a private detective who solves very riveting and has astounding crimes daily. Alexa is a woman full of talents as she is not only talented at what she does but also blunt, quite empathetic, not a perfect stirrer, but still wraps all this up with a sense of humor to die for.

Little is known of Alexa apart from the fact that she is a widow, something which sees her get termed aa as a “mystery”, When Detective Kieran Hussey, one of Alexa’s long-time allies reaches out for her help on some of his “hard” cases, Alexa plays reluctant but see inside she is dying with excitement. With the sarcastic and ground data analyst joining the team, Alexa is close to unstoppable.

My Life is Murder Season 4 Cast: Who will appear?

Lucy Lawless as Alexa is like the backbone of the crime series, and now that the series is renewed for a fourth season, she will be back to reprise her role as Alexa Crowe, the detective. Ebony Vagulans will be back as Madison, the data analyst, Bernard Curry as Detective Kieran, Rawiri Jobe as Harry, Alex Andreas as George, and Joe Naufahu as Reuben. Other casts include Dilruk Jayasinha as Dr. Suresh, Kate McCartney as Dawn, Martin Henderson as Will, Todd River as Captain Thunderbolt, and Zeppelin as Chowder.
