Who is Jerry Springer wife Micki Velton?

Gerald Norman Springer was an American businessman, broadcaster, journalist, actor, producer, lawyer, and politician of British origin.  Springer was Cincinnati’s 56th mayor from 1977 until 1978. Between September 30, 1991, to July 26, 2018, he hosted the tabloid talk show Jerry Springer, and in 2015, he debuted the Jerry Springer Podcast. Springer presented America’s Got Talent from 2007 to 2008, and the courtroom show Judge Jerry from September 2019 to 2022.

Who is Jerry Springer wife Micki Velton?

Micki Velton and Jerry Springer first met on a blind date in 1969 and married in 1973.

Springer was elected to the Cincinnati City Council at the time, a position he resigned from in 1974 after admitting to hiring a prostitute. However, the controversy definitely did not destabilize his partnership. Spring and Velton had been married for more than two decades before divorcing in 1912.

However, It’s unclear why the couple separated, although it happened around the time Springer’s TV career took off. Both parties have remained silent since they parted ways. Nevertheless, Springer never married again.

Meanwhile, Springer went on to work as a political campaign strategist for Democrat Robert F. Kennedy. After Kennedy’s assassination, he began practicing law at Frost & Jacobs, now Frost Brown Todd, in Cincinnati.

From 1973 through 1985, Springer was a partner in the law firm of Grinker, Sudman & Springer, alongside former NBA agent Ronnie Grinker (d. 1997) and current Butler County, Ohio, magistrate Harry Sudman.
