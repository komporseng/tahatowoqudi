See How Naturi Naughton's Daughter Zuri Celebrated Her 3rd Birthday Amid Quarantine

Lots of balloons and beautiful colors as Naturi Naughton holds fun, Moana- themed birthday party for 3-year old Zuri.

The "Power" star recently took to social media shower love on her adorable daughter Zuri and to celebrate her birthday, declaring her age and gushing over the baby girl.

Naturi Naughton visited "Extra" at The Levi's Store Times Square on October 10, 2019 in New York City | Photo: Getty Images

Naughton also shared cute pics and a video of the special party organized to mark the third birthday of her daughter--- a tropical/Moana themed affair which seemed like a lot of fun,despite quarantine restrictions.

Naughton recently shared how challenging it was to get the role of Tasha St Patrick, wife of Power star, Omari Hardwick who played the role of James St Patrick.

There were pictures of Zuri having with few friends in a beautiful turquoise Moana-styled bathing suit and of course, her cake-- also turquoise, displaying just her name and a Moana logo. As reported by People, the actress, along with her longtime boyfriend, Ben welcomed their baby girl on Wednesday, July 19,2017.

Naughton had disclosed at the time that although the pregnancy had come as a surprise to the couple,she had been very excited and was looking forward to entering what she considered a new stage of her life.

As it turned out, Naughton and Ben broke up not long after, with the actress realizing that the father of her 3-year old daughter didn't fit the bill for the future she wanted:

''I don't want to be married if it's not the right marriage and the right person ''.

While the "Lottery Ticket" star felt very strongly about the break up as revealed in her song which she titled 'Stay too long', she confirmed that Ben wasn't going to be completely out of Zuri's life as the actress disclosed that she and Ben would be co-parenting.

In addition to that, BET noted that the actress had struggled for a long time in her relationship as problems began to come up after she got pregnant. The actress admitted that she had struggled with fears and unrealistic expectations in her love live, after which she decided to let go:

''I made a difficult decision to end my relationship because I had to put my needs first''.

As reported by Express, Naughton recently shared how challenging it was to get the role of Tasha St Patrick, wife of Power star, Omari Hardwick who played the role of James St Patrick, a successful drug dealer whose empire was on the verge of crumbling.
