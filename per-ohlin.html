Per Ohlin- Biography

Per Ohlin was born in Sweden on January 16, 1969. Best remembered as the frontman of the popular Norwegian-based metal band Mayhem, he also established a short-lived Swedish group called Morbid. He performed under the stage name “Dead.” He and Mikael Akerfeldt were both well-known, Swedish-born death metal vocalists. As a child, he was a victim of severe bullying; he nearly died when his spleen ruptured following an episode of physical violence.
