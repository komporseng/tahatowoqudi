Who is Steve Howe:

Steve Howe is a famous Guitarist. He was born on April 8, 1947 and his birthplace is England. Steve is also well known as, English musician most famous as the guitarist and backup singer for the prog rock band Yes. He is also a prolific solo artist, recording nineteen solo albums from 1975 to 2010.

Steve is originated from England. He replaced guitarist Peter Banks in Yes in 1970.
